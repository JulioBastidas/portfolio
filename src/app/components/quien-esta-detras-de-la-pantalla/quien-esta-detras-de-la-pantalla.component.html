¿Quién esta detras de la pantalla?

Made on april 25 - 2019

Workshop III: Interactive audiovisual narratives

Audiovisual interactive Project about Alfredo who meets Amaya through the internet but one day in the middle of a game she disappears leaving him clues to find her. The story has 3 different finals and it depends of the decision the user takes.

The interactions between the video are remembering the patron as the circles light up in the screen, rotate the lines in the correct order to connect the path between the 2 triangles and click on the circles as soon as they appear in the screen.
